Bitcoin has evolved from obscurity to prominence in recent years, marked by a meteoric price surge in 2017 and increased institutional adoption thereafter. Regulatory clarity has improved gradually, and Bitcoin's narrative shifted from speculation to recognition as a store of value, cementing its role in global finance.

DogeCoin, originally created as a lighthearted joke, has evolved into a widely recognized cryptocurrency.
